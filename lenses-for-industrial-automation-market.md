<p><p>Lenses for industrial automation are categorized primarily into CCD and CMOS industrial camera lenses. CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) lenses are known for their superior image quality and low-light performance, making them suitable for applications requiring high sensitivity and precise imaging. In contrast, CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) lenses offer faster processing speeds and lower power consumption, which is ideal for real-time applications. Both types are essential in various industries, including manufacturing, quality control, and robotics, facilitating automated inspection and monitoring processes.</p></p>

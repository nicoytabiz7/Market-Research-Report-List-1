<p><p>The TDLAS (Tunable Diode Laser Absorption Spectroscopy) gas analyzer market is divided into two primary types: in situ laser analyzers and extractive laser analyzers. In situ laser analyzers are designed to measure gas concentrations directly within the process environment, providing real-time data without sample alteration. In contrast, extractive laser analyzers involve collecting gas samples from the source, which are then analyzed in a separate measurement chamber, allowing for precise calibration and analysis of gas components.</p></p>

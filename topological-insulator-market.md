<p><p>The topological insulator market encompasses three key types: Weak Topological Insulators (WTIs), which exhibit protected surface states but are less stable than other variants; Topological Crystal Insulators (TCIs), characterized by their crystal symmetry leading to unique surface states that can be manipulated; and High-order Topological Insulators (HOTIs), which feature higher-dimensional surface states, offering novel quantum phenomena. Each type presents distinct properties and applications, driving innovation in electronics, spintronics, and quantum computing technologies.</p></p>

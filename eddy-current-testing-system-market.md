<p><p>Eddy Current Testing is a non-destructive testing (NDT) method that utilizes electromagnetic induction to detect surface and near-surface flaws in conductive materials. This technique is widely used in various industries including aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing, primarily for its accuracy and ability to inspect complex geometries.</p><p>The Eddy Current Testing System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.3% during the forecast period.

<p><p>The Catalyst Coated Membrane Electrode Assemblies (MEAs) market consists of two main types: 3-layer MEAs and 5-layer MEAs. </p><p>3-layer MEAs typically include a membrane sandwiched between two catalyst-coated layers, suitable for various applications due to their simplicity and efficiency. </p><p>5-layer MEAs add additional layers, enhancing performance by optimizing catalyst distribution and improving mass transport, leading to higher efficiency in fuel cells. Both types are crucial in advancing fuel cell technology and improving energy conversion efficiencies.</p></p>

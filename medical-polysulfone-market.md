<p><p>The medical polysulfone market encompasses three primary types: Polysulfone (PSU), Polyethersulfone (PES), and Polyphenylsulfone (PPSU). PSU offers excellent thermal stability and biocompatibility, making it suitable for medical devices. PES is known for its superior mechanical properties and resistance to hydrolysis, ideal for applications like filtration membranes. PPSU stands out with remarkable toughness and high-temperature resistance, often used in sterilizable surgical instruments. Together, these materials enhance the functionality and safety of various medical applications.</p></p>

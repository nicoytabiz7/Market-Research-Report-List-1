<p><ul><li>Open Cooling Tower</li><li>Closed Cooling Tower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry cooling tower market is segmented into open and closed cooling towers. Open cooling towers utilize ambient air to cool water through evaporation, making them highly efficient for large-scale industrial applications. In contrast, closed cooling towers maintain a sealed system where water is cooled through a heat exchanger, minimizing water loss and offering better control over water quality. Both types cater to different operational needs, balancing efficiency, maintenance, and environmental considerations for industries like power generation and manufacturing.</p></p>

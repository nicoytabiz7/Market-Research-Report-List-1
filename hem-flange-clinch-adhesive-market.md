<p><p>The Hem Flange (Clinch) Adhesive market is categorized into two main types: One Component and Two Component adhesives. One Component adhesives are single-part formulations that cure upon exposure to air or heat, offering ease of use and lower application complexity. In contrast, Two Component adhesives consist of a resin and a hardener that need to be mixed before application, providing superior bonding strength and flexibility. Both types are essential for applications requiring durable joining solutions in automotive and manufacturing sectors.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Inboard Brake Accumulator</li><li>Outboard Brake Accumulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake accumulator market is categorized into two main types: inboard and outboard brake accumulators. Inboard brake accumulators are typically integrated within the vehicle's chassis, providing space efficiency and improved weight distribution. Conversely, outboard brake accumulators are installed externally, allowing for easier maintenance and replacement. Both types serve to store hydraulic pressure, ensuring reliable braking performance in various conditions. The choice between inboard and outboard accumulators often depends on vehicle design, application, and performance requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>The 5G Drive Telecom Optical Module market encompasses several encapsulation types tailored for high-speed data transmission. SFP/SFP+ encapsulation supports data rates up to 10 Gbps, ideal for short-range applications. XFP encapsulation offers flexibility with up to 10 Gbps, often used in long-haul connections. QSFP/QSFP+ encapsulations enable higher bandwidth, supporting up to 40 Gbps or more, suitable for data centers. CFP encapsulation is designed for 100 Gbps and beyond, essential for backbone networks. Each type addresses specific bandwidth and distance requirements in 5G infrastructure.</p></p>

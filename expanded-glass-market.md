<p><p>Expanded glass, also known as expanded glass beads or expanded glass aggregates, is a lightweight material produced by heating glass to a high temperature until it softens and expands. This process creates a porous structure, making the material an excellent thermal insulator and lightweight aggregate for construction applications. Its applications span across several industries, including construction, automotive, and packaging, due to its excellent insulating properties, resistance to moisture, and versatility.</p><p>The Expanded Glass Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.7% during the forecast period. Additionally, the rising focus on energy efficiency in buildings is propelling the market further, as expanded glass significantly improves insulation.

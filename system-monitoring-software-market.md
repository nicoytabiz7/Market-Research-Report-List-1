<p><p>System monitoring software is categorized into three main market types: On-Premise, PaaS, and SaaS. On-Premise solutions are installed locally on a company’s infrastructure, providing complete control but requiring management of hardware and software updates. PaaS (Platform as a Service) offers a cloud-based framework for developers to build and deploy applications, enabling easier scalability and collaboration. SaaS (Software as a Service) delivers software over the internet, eliminating the need for local installations and simplifying access and maintenance for users.</p></p>

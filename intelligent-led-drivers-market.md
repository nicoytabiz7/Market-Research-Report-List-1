<p><p>Intelligent LED drivers are categorized based on their control and dimming capabilities. 0-10V dimmable drivers use a simple analog signal to adjust brightness. DALI (Digital Addressable Lighting Interface) dimmable drivers allow for digital communication, enabling complex lighting control in multiple zones. DMX (Digital Multiplex) dimmable drivers are primarily used in theatrical and event lighting, providing flexible control over multiple fixtures. Other market types may include proprietary protocols and wireless drivers, enhancing customization and integration within smart lighting systems.</p></p>

In contrast, crawler tillers utilize a tracked system, providing stability and enhanced traction on uneven or soft terrain. This makes them ideal for heavy-duty operations in challenging soil conditions. Both types cater to different agricultural needs, boosting productivity and soil management efficiency.</p></p>

<p><p>The roofing liners market includes various materials used for flat and low-sloped roofing applications. EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) is a durable synthetic rubber known for its weather resistance and longevity. TPO (thermoplastic olefin) features a reflective surface that enhances energy efficiency, while PVC (polyvinyl chloride) offers excellent waterproofing and flame resistance. Other materials may encompass modified bitumen and spray polyurethane foam. Each type serves specific needs in terms of performance, cost, and environmental considerations, catering to diverse roofing requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>The Pilot-operated Relief Valve market is categorized into Closed Type and Non-closed Type designs. Closed Type valves feature a sealed pilot system that prevents fluid from escaping during normal operations, enhancing safety and efficiency. In contrast, Non-closed Type valves allow some fluid venting, providing more flexibility in pressure management but potentially less control over leakage. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, ensuring pressure regulation and safeguarding equipment from overpressure conditions while catering to diverse operational needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The Polymer Filtration Systems market is divided into two primary types: Continuous and Discontinuous systems. Discontinuous systems, on the other hand, function in batches, allowing for periodic filtration and servicing, making them suitable for smaller operations or specific needs. Each system caters to different industries and processes, ensuring optimal performance based on operational requirements and resource availability.</p></p>

<p><p>The molecular imaging agents market comprises various types, including small molecules, which are low molecular weight compounds often used in imaging due to their ability to penetrate tissues. Peptides are short chains of amino acids that can target specific biomolecules. Engineered protein fragments are designed for high specificity and stability in imaging applications. Aptamers are short, single-stranded DNA or RNA molecules that can bind to target molecules. Nanoparticles provide enhanced imaging contrast and targeted delivery, while "others" include emerging and novel agents in research.</p></p>

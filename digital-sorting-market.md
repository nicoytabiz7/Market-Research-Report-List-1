<p><p>The digital sorting market encompasses various technologies used to enhance sorting efficiency in diverse industries. Cameras are used for high-resolution imaging to identify and separate materials based on color and shape. Lasers provide precise measurements for dimensional sorting, while NIR sorters utilize near-infrared spectroscopy to differentiate materials based on composition. Hyperspectral cameras capture a wide spectrum of light, enabling detailed analysis of material properties. Combined sorters integrate multiple technologies for comprehensive sorting solutions, offering improved accuracy and operational flexibility.</p></p>

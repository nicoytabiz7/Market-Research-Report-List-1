<p><strong>The Automobile Electric Power Steering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS</li><li>PEPS</li><li>REPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automobile Electric Power Steering (EPS) Systems market is categorized into three main types: Column Electric Power Steering (CEPS), Pinion Electric Power Steering (PEPS), and Rack Electric Power Steering (REPS). CEPS integrates the motor within the steering column, offering compactness. PEPS places the motor on the steering pinion for better space utilization and performance. REPS features the motor directly on the steering rack, providing enhanced steering feel and performance. Each type caters to different vehicle designs and performance needs.</p></p>

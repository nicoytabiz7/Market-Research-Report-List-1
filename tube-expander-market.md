<p><ul><li>Parallel Tube Expansion</li><li>Flare Tube Expansion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Tube Expander market primarily consists of two types: Parallel Tube Expansion and Flare Tube Expansion. Parallel Tube Expansion involves uniform stretching of tubes, allowing for precise fitting and alignment in various applications, such as HVAC systems. Flare Tube Expansion, on the other hand, involves creating a flared end on the tubes, enhancing the sealing capability and improving connections in fluid systems. Both types cater to industries like oil and gas, automotive, and manufacturing, each serving specific needs for efficient tube joining.</p></p>

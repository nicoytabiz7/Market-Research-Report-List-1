<p><p>The low-floor tram market can be categorized into two primary types: 100% low-floor trams and 70% low-floor trams. </p><p>100% low-floor trams feature a fully flat passenger deck, providing seamless access for users with mobility challenges, such as those using wheelchairs or strollers. This design enhances accessibility and convenience for all passengers. </p><p>In contrast, 70% low-floor trams have a portion of their floors raised, typically towards the middle, which may limit accessibility slightly but can still accommodate a range of passengers while optimizing design and manufacturing costs.</p></p>

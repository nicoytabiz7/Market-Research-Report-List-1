<p><p>Hydronic heating systems utilize water to transfer heat efficiently in residential and commercial spaces. Key market types include radiant floor heating, which provides warmth directly through the flooring; radiator heating, where hot water circulates through radiators that emit heat into the room; baseboard heating, which involves hot water flowing through baseboard units to warm the air; and hydronic air heating, where heated water warms air in ducts. Each type offers unique advantages in comfort and energy efficiency.</p></p>

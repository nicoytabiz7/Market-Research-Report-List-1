<p><p>The special rubber market encompasses various types tailored for specific applications. Nitrile rubber excels in oil resistance, making it ideal for automotive and industrial seals. Fluorine rubber offers exceptional chemical resistance, often used in aerospace and chemical processing. Butyl rubber is renowned for airtight properties, commonly utilized in tire linings. Silicone rubber withstands extreme temperatures, suitable for medical and cookware applications. Acrylate rubber provides outstanding weather resistance and is used in outdoor seals. Other specialty rubbers address niche needs across diverse industries.</p></p>

<p><p>Lysosomal Storage Diseases (LSDs) target therapies include Enzyme Replacement Therapy (ERT), Substrate Reduction Therapy (SRT), and Cystine Depleting Agents. ERT delivers essential enzymes to compensate for deficiencies, effectively managing symptoms in conditions like Gaucher and Fabry diseases. SRT minimizes the substrates that accumulate due to enzyme deficiencies, aiming to slow disease progression. Cystine Depleting Agents reduce cystine accumulation in disorders like cystinosis, promoting better organ function. Together, these therapies address the diverse needs of LSD patients.</p></p>

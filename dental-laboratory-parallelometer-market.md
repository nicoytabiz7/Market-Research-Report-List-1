<p><p>The dental laboratory parallelometer market is segmented into two primary types: 1-arm and 2-arm parallelometers. The 1-arm parallelometer features a single arm that supports the positioning of dental casts for accurate measurement and alignment, making it suitable for basic applications. In contrast, the 2-arm parallelometer includes two adjustable arms, allowing for enhanced precision and versatility in complex dental restorations. This type is preferred for intricate designs, as it provides better stability and alignment during the dental fabrication process.</p></p>

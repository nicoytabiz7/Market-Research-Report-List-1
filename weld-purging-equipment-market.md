<p><strong>The Weld Purging Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Weld Purge Monitor</li><li>Weld Purging System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The weld purging equipment market primarily consists of two types: weld purge monitors and weld purging systems. Weld purge monitors are devices used to measure the oxygen content in the atmosphere around a weld, ensuring optimal conditions for quality welds. Weld purging systems, on the other hand, are integrated setups designed to create a controlled inert gas environment around the weld joint, minimizing oxidation and contamination. Both types are essential for enhancing weld quality and efficiency in industries like aerospace and automotive.</p></p>

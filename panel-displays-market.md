<p><p>The panel display market primarily includes Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED) and Liquid Crystal Display (LCD) technologies. OLEDs utilize organic compounds that emit light when electricity is applied, resulting in vibrant colors and deep blacks, making them ideal for high-end applications. In contrast, LCDs use liquid crystals illuminated by backlighting, offering good image quality at a lower cost. Both technologies cater to various sectors, including consumer electronics, automotive, and commercial displays, reflecting diverse consumer preferences and technological advancements.</p></p>

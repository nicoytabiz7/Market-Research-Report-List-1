<p><strong>The Aviation Actuation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Drive System</li><li>Electric Drive System</li><li>Pneumatic Drive System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aviation actuation systems are essential for controlling various aircraft functions, divided into three main market types: hydraulic, electric, and pneumatic drive systems. Hydraulic drive systems utilize fluid pressure to operate mechanisms, offering high force in compact designs, commonly used in landing gear and flaps. Electric drive systems leverage electric motors for actuation, providing precise control and lower weight, ideal for modern fly-by-wire systems. Pneumatic drive systems use compressed air, often found in lighter applications, allowing for quick response and reliability in specific flight controls.</p></p>

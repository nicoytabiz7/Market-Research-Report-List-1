<p><p>An Arteriovenous (AV) fistula needle is a specialized medical device used primarily in hemodialysis to access blood vessels for the purpose of filtering blood in patients with kidney failure. These needles are designed to facilitate the flow of blood from the patient to a dialysis machine and back, ensuring efficient and safe treatment. The AV fistula is created by surgically connecting an artery to a vein, allowing for repeated access over time.</p><p>The AV fistula needle market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing prevalence of chronic kidney diseases worldwide and the rising number of patients requiring renal replacement therapy. Technological advancements in needle design, such as improved ergonomics and reduced trauma, are enhancing patient experiences and encouraging adoption among healthcare providers.

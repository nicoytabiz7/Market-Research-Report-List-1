<p><ul><li>UV curing</li><li>Visible light-curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dual-curing adhesives combine both UV and visible light curing technologies, enabling enhanced versatility and efficiency in bonding applications. UV curing uses ultraviolet light to initiate polymerization, providing rapid hardening under direct light exposure. In contrast, visible light-curing relies on a broader spectrum of light, allowing for curing in various environments and deeper penetration in opaque materials. This dual mechanism ensures effective adhesion in challenging conditions, making these adhesives popular in industries like dentistry, automotive, and electronics for their reliability and speed.</p></p>

<p><p>Pick to Light systems are warehouse technologies that streamline order fulfillment by utilizing visual indicators to direct pickers to the correct items. The market types include Manual systems, where operators follow lights to locate items, enhancing picking speed with minimal training. In contrast, Auto Guided systems use automated vehicles equipped with lights for a hands-free approach, improving efficiency in larger warehouses. Both systems aim to reduce errors and increase productivity by simplifying the picking process through visual cues.</p></p>
